Our story

Povestea Noastra

  • 2014

    First We Meet

    They saw each other and this moment will go down in history. A look was all it took. However, the story was not about to unfold just yet.

  • 02 Septembrie 2016

    First Date

    Funny how life is. A game of chance brought them together again, two years later. A romantic dinner was the first step in their love story. But both of them wanted to be sure.

  • 14 Septembrie 2016

    In A Relationship

    So it begins. After some more dates they were sure. Two pieces of the same puzzle coming together. Natural. And while they were runners before, so they started running together.

  • 02 Septembrie 2018

    We're Engaged

    Fast forwarding two years later and travelling the world together, she said yes. On a secluded beach they decided to move forward in life, as husband and wife. One step forward in this marathon of life. But while they used to run for competition, now they run together to enjoy it. In the end, that's how you know you are where you were supposed to be all along.
